The Cake Lab
A smart app that provides fulfillment of collaboration, unique experience, and reduce unpredictability to having a memorable and smooth cake customizing experience.

Overview
What it is?
A mobile APP for customers to build their ideal customizing cake.
Problems
The value of customization is very high and it can increase customer satisfaction to help the business stand out. However, getting everything right is challenge.
Generating a customized cake idea is challenging
It’s hard to get inspirations to make a unique cake that match the person’s personality and things they love.
The communication cost is high
It’s hard to make sure the cake maker and orderer are on the same page in design until seeing the outcome.
The cake-customizing process is too complicated
2/3 customers said they need to use multiple platforms to complete the whole customizing process
Target Users
Women from 25-34 years old. (they take 40.18% of all customized cake market while 29.4% from 18-24.)
Persona
29 years Lives in Manhattan, NY Prefers to shop online Cares about shop reputation and quality
User Goals
Wants to order a unique cake to surprise her friend for her birthday Wants to know have more control of the process to make sure everything goes well Wants to find a shop that fit with her expectation and can trust
Frustrations
Feel hard to have a clear concept of the cake Feel hard to explain her ideas to the maker Can’t make sure the they are on the same page The overall process is conplicated
User Journey
”
HMW provide a predictable, simple, and creative way for Gen Z customers to customize cake?
User Testing
We tested with 3 people to make the experience more intutive.

Iteraction 1

Information in the home page isn’t clear enough
‍
The “keep Finishing” and “Previous Design” are a bit overlapping. The available time slots are hidden and are easy to be missed.

Iteraction 2

The tools don’t feel customizable enough
‍
It’s hard to make sure the cake maker and orderer are on the same page in design until seeing the outcome.

Iteraction 3

The experience of collaborative card is confusing
‍
2/3 customers said they need to use multiple platforms to complete the whole customizing process

Solutions
Onboarding & inspiration
Providing inspirations based on what you upload about the person who you build the cake for through AI technology.
Design the unique cake like a pro
By using the 3D app, you will see the price according to what you are adding to the design. The Cake Lab will also create the cake you design accurately through the 3D model to reduce the gap of communication.
Viewed in 3D
By seeing your design in the real place with the AR function, you will never struggle with if the design fit the party vibe or not.
Check out with transparency and collaborate for showing your love
By following the check out flow, the progress of the cake becomes more predictable. Customers can also collaborate on the card creation to show your love to your loved one.
